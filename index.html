About Us

Fleet Tec LLC has been a trusted partner in heavy-duty truck and equipment repair since our inception. We specialize in providing reliable and efficient solutions for private and commercial fleets, ensuring your vehicles are always ready for the road.

Your Fleet, Our Expertise

At Fleet Tec LLC, we take pride in offering comprehensive repair services, from heavy-duty truck repair to hydraulic equipment rebuilds and mobile diesel mechanic services. With a team of skilled technicians and years of industry experience, we handle everything from minor fixes to major overhauls. Whether it's boom inspections, welding, fabrication, or preventative maintenance, we’ve got you covered.

Our Services

Heavy Duty Truck Repair

Comprehensive repair solutions for all makes and models of heavy-duty trucks, handling both minor and major repairs with unmatched expertise.

Hydraulic & Boom Repair

Expert repair and rebuild services for hydraulic systems and boom equipment, ensuring optimal performance and safety compliance.

Mobile Diesel Mechanic
